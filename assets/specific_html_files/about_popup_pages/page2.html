Ski Pose Detection

During my participation in the IBU-IOC Virtual Camps 2020, organized by the International Biathlon Union (IBU), I attended a technique analysis session led by Stefan Lindinger. In this session, I noticed that each study relied on expensive sensors—not accessible to every team. Therefore, I decided to spend the remaining afternoons exploring the application of different AI-based strategies for pose detection and sharing my findings with coaches from various national teams, receiving positive feedback from them.

Click here for the GitHub Repo.

Since it was a summer camp, I recorded myself training on rollerskis—the summer adaptation of skiing—for the demo.
