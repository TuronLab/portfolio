The EMPHASIS project, which stands for "Empowering Decision Making with Higher Productivity by Means of HyperAutomation," is an initiative dedicated to advancing the hyperautomation of back-office tasks through cutting-edge AI technologies, including Natural Language Processing (NLP) and speech processing. The project's primary goal is to support companies in their digital transformation by creating a versatile platform that can process unstructured data—such as documents and audio from calls—and convert it into structured, actionable information.

My team and I contributed to the development of the NLP components of the project, focusing on building key technological modules for Cognitive Document Automation (CDA). These modules were designed to be adaptable across various use cases and languages, aligning with the project's low-code/no-code philosophy.

Our main areas of development included:

  • Text and Document Classification: We developed tools for extracting multi-format documents, fine-tuning Large Language Models (LLMs), and automatically classifying texts and documents. This enabled the system to categorize items such as invoices, delivery notes, and building permits, significantly streamlining their processing.
  • Named Entity Recognition and Anonymization (NERC): We created a module to identify and classify specific entities within a text—such as names, locations, and other sensitive information. A key feature of this module is its anonymization capability, which replaces detected entities in a configurable way to ensure data privacy.
  • Question Answering (QA): We developed a module that allows users to extract answers from large corpora of text. It supports three search modes: semantic similarity, extractive QA, and lexical search, making it a powerful tool for information retrieval in environments like FAQs.

These modules were implemented as REST APIs and integrated into the EMPHASIS platform, where they can be combined into configurable processing pipelines. Our work on these components delivered a flexible and powerful hyperautomation solution, capable of adapting to the unique needs of different clients and domains.
